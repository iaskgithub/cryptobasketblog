## Introduction
Radio Caca is a decentralized organization (DAO) operated by internet-native individuals all over the globe who share a single vision of building a brave new virtual world. Ticker: RACA. and is currently deployed on Ethereum, Solana, OKExChain, and BNB Chain. Radio Caca has a native token.
